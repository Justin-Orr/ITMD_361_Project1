A little bit about me

I was born in Flint, Michigan and lived with my grandmother for some time. Then at the age of 3, I moved to Park Forest, Illinois to live with my dad along with my older brother Robert.

Park Forest and Chicago Heights is where I spent my childhood and built my relationships with my dad's side of the family. I attended Wilson Elementary then moved on to Bloom Township Highschool at the age of 15.

After finishing my first year there I was accepted into the Illinois Mathematics and Science Academy in Aurora Illinois and finished my highschool education there.

Moving forward from IMSA I attended the Illinois Institute of Technology in Chicago Illinois studying Computer Engineering. Midway through I switched to Computer Science and here I am now in my last semester looking to graduate this winter 2020.

Hobbies

One of my favorite hobbies is to do photography. I started back in highschool during my sophomore year and put in a recorded 1,500 hours of work for the school. I use a Nikon 3500 DSLR with a 70-200mm lens. I love talking about camera quality and capabilities and am always open to learning about new camera techniques.

I am coffee enthusiast and love to see coffee being made. However, the way I drink my coffee does not match my love for it. I drink it black with no extra ingredients. I love to play video games and watch shows like The Peaky Blinders or Game of Thrones.

One other hobby, which has flooded my youtube recommendations recently, is woodworking and DIY projects. Finally, camping is becoming one of my new interests and love to hear about cool things you can do while camping.
